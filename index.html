2025–2026 Recap

What we did last year

Here's a look at what South Mecklenburg FBLA accomplished — this is what you're joining.

🥇
State Medalists
Members won medals at the NC State Leadership Conference in Greensboro, competing against hundreds of students from across the state.
🏟️
Competed at RLC
The chapter competed at Marvin Ridge High School for the Regional Leadership Conference — workshops, judges, and prizes included.
✈️
Traveled to States
3 days at the Sheraton Four Seasons in Greensboro. Competitions, networking, and memories the chapter won't forget.
🎤
Management Consulting Speaker
Benjamin Lowe, a Partner at Roland Berger, led an interactive LinkedIn workshop for the chapter on energy transition careers.
📚
IKM Book Drive
Members donated Pre-K through elementary books to IKM. Every book = 1 service hour. Drop-offs ran through December.
🎉
End-of-Year Trivia Showdown
5 rounds, 4 teams, one champion. The winning team took home 3 service hours at the final meeting of the year.

Get Involved

Join South Mecklenburg FBLA

Here's everything you need to know to get started for 2026–2027.

1

Come to the first meeting

Meetings are the 2nd and 4th Monday of each month in room X-2018. Morning and afternoon sessions — attend whichever works.

2

Fill out the Google Form

Sign in and join the GroupMe for all updates. Link shared at the meeting.

3

Pay $16 dues on OSP

Pay early — whoever pays first gets first pick of their competitive event for the RLC.

4

Pick your event

Talk to Mr. Ivy in X-2018 or email him. Study materials will be provided once you're signed up.

5

Compete & have fun

RLC in the fall, States in the spring in Greensboro. School absences for States are excused.
